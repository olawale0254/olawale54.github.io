Who We Are

Exito Petroleum Ltd is a wholly indigenous energy company incorporated in Nigeria with an ardent interest in the upstream, midstream and downstream global energy market.

Exito Petroleum was established with the view to provide effective solutions to the downstream and upstream sub sector of the Oil and Gas Industry. We are positioned to take up Importation, Trading and Distribution of Petroleum products, Procurement & Supply of Marine equipment, Marine Logistics and Support services.

Established in 2014, Exito Petroleum is led by seasoned professional with International expertise and combined experience of 20 years in Product Importation, Trading and Distribution, Marine Logistics and Offshore Support Services.

Our knowledge and expertise is valued by our clients and our ability to attract, develop and retain exceptional talent underpins our success.

At Exito Petroleum, we adhere to global operational standards guiding the delivery of our services as well as operational laws of the regions we do business. Exito Petroleum strive to be the apex indigenous company.
